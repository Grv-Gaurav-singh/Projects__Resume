Purpose: Implementing a Power BI dashboard for HR data analytics to streamline processes and centralize employee data tracking.

Results:
Increased Efficiency: Saves 3-4 hours of HR work daily by automating data gathering and analysis.
Better Decision-Making: Provides real-time insights for informed HR decisions.
Improved Employee Management: Facilitates monitoring of attendance, performance, and leave records.
Compliance and Reporting: Generates custom reports for policy adherence and stakeholder communication.
Enhanced Employee Experience: Improves interactions with HR through efficient processes.

A dual-axis chart is a chart representing two sets of data overlaid on top of one another, in which the resulting charts will have a shared axis.
